About CalorieTracker

Helping you make smarter health choices, one meal at a time.

Our Mission

CalorieTracker was built to make nutrition and fitness tracking accessible and simple. Whether you're trying to lose weight, build muscle, manage a condition, or just eat better — we're here to support you with a clean and powerful interface.

What You Can Do

  • 🔍 Track your daily calories & macronutrients
  • 🍎 Log meals, exercises, water, biometrics & notes
  • 📊 View trends over time to improve your health
  • 🎯 Set goals and monitor progress easily
