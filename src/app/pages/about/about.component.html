🥗 About CalorieTracker

Your personal assistant for smarter, healthier living.

🚀 Our Mission

At CalorieTracker, our goal is simple: make nutrition & fitness tracking effortless. Whether you're losing weight, building muscle, or just living healthier — we’ve got your back.

✨ What You Can Do

  • 🔍 Track daily calories & macronutrients
  • 🍎 Log meals, exercises, water, biometrics & notes
  • 📊 Visualize trends to optimize your habits
  • 🎯 Set goals & stay motivated
